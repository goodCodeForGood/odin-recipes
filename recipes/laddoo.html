Coconut Laddoo

Description

This is the recipe for Coconut Laddoo, an Indian dessert. Coconut laddoos are healthy, highly nutritious, and perfect for special occasions. The preparation time for our recipe is 30 minutes, and the cooking time is 15 minutes. Follow our easy recipe, which can yield 26 laddoos, and enjoy the dessert!

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces unsweetened flaked coconut
  • Â½ teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 3 drops orange food coloring (Optional)
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon rose water (Optional)

Steps

  1. Combine coconut and cardamom in a pan over medium heat. Mix well and heat for 1 minute, but do not let coconut brown.
  2. Add food coloring. Add condensed milk. Cook and stir until coconut begins to stick together, 5 to 8 minutes.
  3. Add rose water; cook and stir until flavors blend, 8 to 10 minutes more.
  4. Let mixture cool until safe to handle, but still warm. Roll mixture into balls
  5. Place laddoos in cupcake liners or on a baking sheet. Serve immediately or refrigerate up to a week.
